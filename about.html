  • High Volume

    Most airlines allow extra, overweight, and oversize baggage for additional fees. That lets us move large quantities of goods to their intended destinations fast. Additionally, we can also offer insurance for high-value items.

  • Fastest Delivery

    Hand Carry is the fastest way to get your consignments to their international destinations. By using scheduled flights, you don’t have to wait for international charter flight permits. Since baggage is the last thing loaded onto the aircraft and the first thing unloaded, we need less time between connections. That makes hand carry faster than Express Airfreight.

  • Personally Escorted

    Our onboard Courier personally escorts your shipment. The OBC is physically present at all times to handle any issues that may arise. The courier can act as the shipper’s or consignee’s representative at customs, assisting with expediting the customs clearance process.

  • First & Last Mile

    We can arrange the first and last mile transportation for your consignments, providing white-glove door-to-door service, alleviating your headaches, and getting your shipments to their destinations quickly and safely.
